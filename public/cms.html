You can use basic HTML tags for formatting, e.g.:

  • <p>Paragraph text</p>
  • <h2>Section Heading</h2>
  • <strong>Bold text</strong> or <b>Bold text</b>
  • <em>Italic text</em> or <i>Italic text</i>
  • <ul><li>List item</li></ul> for bullet lists
  • <ol><li>List item</li></ol> for numbered lists
  • <blockquote>Quoted text</blockquote>

Do NOT include <script> tags.
